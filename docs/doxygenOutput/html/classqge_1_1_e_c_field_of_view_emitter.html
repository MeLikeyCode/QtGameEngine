Detailed Description

An entity controller that checks the field of view of the controlled entity and emits a signal whenever other entities enter or leave the controlled entity's field of view.

You can also use entitiesInView() to get all the current entities in the controlled entity's field of view.

TODO: By default, ECFieldOfViewEmitter will emit a signal when any entity enters the controlled entity's field of view, but you can have it only emit signals when certain entities of interest enters the field of view of the controlled entity.

Example usage:

ECCheckFOV* c = new ECCheckFOV(entity);
connect(c,&ECCheckFOV::entityEntersFOV,this,myCallback);
connect(c,&ECCheckFOV::entityLeavesFOV,this,myCallback);
