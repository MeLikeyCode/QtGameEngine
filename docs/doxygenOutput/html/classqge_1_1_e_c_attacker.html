Detailed Description

An EntityController that makes the controlled entity attack certain other entities within its field of view.

Currently the attack policy is: The controlled Entity will use the Weapon in its first WeaponSlot. If the controlled entity does not have any WeaponSlots with a Weapon, it will simply not attack. But later, I can make the attack policy settable somehow (maybe via strategy pattern).

Example usage:

ECAttackEnemiesWithWeapon* c = new ECAttackEnemiesWithWeapon(entity);

That is all. From then on, the controlled entity will use its first viable weapon to attack certain entities in its field of view.
