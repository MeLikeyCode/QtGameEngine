◆ frequency()

double frequency ( double  stepSize,
double  targetRate 
)

Given a step size and a target rate, will tell you the frequency needed to get the target rate.

I.e. if my character moves 10 pixels at a time (stepSize) and I want him to move 50 pixels per second (targetRate), how often (in seconds) should I move him? I.e. if my character rotates 10 degrees at a time (stepSize) and I want him to rotate at 50 degrees per second, how often (in seconds) should I move him? Obviously, you need to make sure you are consistent in your units.
